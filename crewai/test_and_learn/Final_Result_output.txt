The research findings highlight the increasing significance of AI in Quality Assurance (QA) processes. AI is described as a game-changer for QA engineers, enabling them to enhance testing processes, efficiency, and accuracy. The trends for 2024 in Quality Engineering include the integration of AI and machine learning (ML) into testing practices, allowing for more effective collaboration between QA engineers and developers. Embracing AI and machine learning tools is identified as a key trend in software quality engineering. Automation testing trends for 2024 also emphasize the importance of AI, with a focus on enhancing test automation, predictive analysis for defect prevention, and natural language processing. Overall, the research points to AI as a transformative force in Quality Engineering, driving innovation, efficiency, and improved quality assurance practices.
